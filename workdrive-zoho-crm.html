Overview

WorkDrive Extension For Zoho CRM

The WorkDrive for Zoho CRM enables CRM users to view, create, update, rename, delete, and share all files, folders, and nested sub-folders associated with records through a widget and workflow for all standard and custom modules.

Attachments from your respective CRM records can be synced and stored into your associated WorkDrive folder. By integrating WorkDrive with Zoho CRM, you can perform day-to-day operations inside Zoho CRM without going to WorkDrive.

Folders are created using workflow triggers and can add multiple folder structures based on specific requirements. You don't need to create folders on record create—you can set up workflows and define how folders are created and where to save the ID for further use.
