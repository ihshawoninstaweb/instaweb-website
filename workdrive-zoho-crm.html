The Problem

Documents live in WorkDrive. CRM lives elsewhere. Your team lives in both.

Sales and service teams waste hours every week switching between Zoho CRM and WorkDrive. They search for files, forget to attach documents to records, and lose track of which version is current.

This disconnect creates confusion, delays, and frustrated customers. Important documents get missed during handoffs. Team members can't find what they need when they need it.

WorkDrive for Zoho CRM bridges this gap by bringing file management directly into your CRM workflow. Everything stays connected, organized, and instantly accessible.

Without integration

  • Constant tab-switching between apps
  • Files not linked to CRM records
  • Version confusion and duplicates
  • Lost documents during handoffs
  • Wasted time searching for files

With integration

  • Files accessible inside CRM records
  • Automatic linking and organization
  • Single source of truth for documents
  • Seamless team collaboration
  • Faster response to customer needs
Features

Everything you need for seamless file management.

In-CRM File Browser

Browse your entire WorkDrive structure without leaving Zoho CRM. Navigate folders, search files, and access documents directly from deal, contact, or account records.

The file browser supports quick filters, recent files, and favorites for instant access to frequently used documents. No more memorizing file paths or hunting through nested folders.

One-Click File Attachment

Attach any WorkDrive file to CRM records with a single click. Files automatically link to the relevant deal, contact, or account for complete context.

Attachments maintain live connections to source filesâ€”updates in WorkDrive reflect automatically in CRM. Everyone always sees the latest version.

Document Preview

Preview documents directly inside CRM without downloading. View PDFs, spreadsheets, presentations, and images in a built-in viewer.

Preview speeds up workflows by eliminating download-wait-open cycles. Review contracts, proposals, and specs instantly during calls or meetings.

Automatic Folder Creation

Automatically create WorkDrive folders when new deals or accounts are created. Pre-defined templates ensure consistent organization across your team.

Folders can include standard documents like contracts, onboarding checklists, and service agreements. New team members start with everything they need.

Version Control & History

Track document versions and access revision history directly from CRM. See who made changes, when, and what was modified.

Version control prevents confusion from multiple copies and ensures everyone works from approved documents. Audit trails support compliance requirements.

Team Collaboration

Comment on files, share with team members, and collaborate without leaving CRM. @mention colleagues to request input or approval.

Collaboration features keep discussions connected to relevant records. Context is preserved, and nothing gets lost in email chains.
