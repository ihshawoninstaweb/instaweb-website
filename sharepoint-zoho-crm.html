Access SharePoint libraries, document sets, and files directly from Zoho CRM records. Bridge the gap between your enterprise content management and customer relationship workflows.

Key features

Enterprise-grade document integration.

  • Browse SharePoint sites and libraries from CRM
  • Link documents to deals, contacts, and accounts
  • Respect SharePoint permissions and security
  • Version history and check-in/check-out support
  • Metadata sync and search capabilities
The Challenge

Enterprise teams use SharePoint. Sales teams live in CRM. Documents get lost in between.

Organizations standardize on SharePoint for document management, compliance, and security. But sales and service teams working in Zoho CRM can't easily access those documents without leaving their workflow.

This disconnect slows down deals, frustrates users, and creates compliance risks. Important contracts, proposals, and client documents end up in personal folders instead of the official repository.

SharePoint for Zoho CRM solves this by embedding SharePoint functionality directly into your CRM. Your team gets enterprise document management without leaving their primary work environment.

Common pain points

  • Sales can't find approved templates
  • Documents stored outside SharePoint
  • Compliance and audit risks
  • Duplicate versions across systems
  • Slow response to document requests

With integration

  • Instant access to approved documents
  • All files stored in SharePoint
  • Full compliance and audit trails
  • Single source of truth maintained
  • Faster document delivery to clients
Features

Enterprise document management, seamlessly integrated.

SharePoint Site Browser

Navigate your SharePoint site structure directly from Zoho CRM. Access team sites, communication sites, and document libraries without leaving your CRM records.

The browser respects your SharePoint navigation and hierarchy, making it intuitive for users already familiar with your SharePoint environment.

Document Linking

Link SharePoint documents to any CRM recordâ€”deals, contacts, accounts, or custom modules. Links maintain live connections to source documents.

When documents update in SharePoint, CRM reflects the changes automatically. Everyone always accesses the current, approved version.

Permission Inheritance

SharePoint permissions carry over to CRM integration. Users only see documents they're authorized to access in SharePoint.

Security compliance is maintained without duplicate permission management. IT teams retain full control over document access.

Version Control

Access full version history for linked documents. See who made changes, when, and what was modified. Restore previous versions when needed.

Check-in/check-out functionality prevents conflicting edits. Teams collaborate on documents without overwriting each other's work.

Metadata & Search

View and edit SharePoint metadata from CRM. Search documents by metadata properties, not just filenames.

Powerful search finds documents based on client name, document type, date ranges, and custom properties. No more hunting through folder structures.

Document Templates

Access approved SharePoint templates directly from CRM workflows. Generate contracts, proposals, and agreements using standardized templates.

Templates ensure consistency and compliance across your team. New hires produce professional documents from day one.
