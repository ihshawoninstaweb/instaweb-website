The Integration Challenge

Your business runs on multiple systems. Your CRM should connect them all.

Most businesses use 10+ software tools dailyâ€”website, eCommerce platform, accounting software, email marketing, customer support, project management, and more. When these systems don't talk to each other, data gets siloed, manual entry multiplies, and opportunities slip through cracks.

Zoho CRM integration creates a unified operating layer where data flows automatically between systems. Leads from your website appear instantly in CRM. Orders from your store create customer records and invoices. Payments update deal stages. Support tickets link to account history.

The result: one source of truth, zero manual data entry, and complete visibility into every customer interaction across your entire business.

Integration Services

Comprehensive connectivity for your entire tech stack.

01

Website Integration

Connect your website to Zoho CRM so every form submission, chat inquiry, and callback request becomes a tracked lead or contact. We integrate WordPress, Webflow, custom sites, and landing page builders.

Integration includes form mapping, lead source tracking, UTM parameter capture, auto-assignment rules, and instant notification workflows. Your sales team never misses an inbound opportunity.

02

eCommerce Integration

Sync your online store with Zoho CRM to create complete customer profiles with order history, lifetime value, and purchase patterns. We integrate Shopify, WooCommerce, Magento, BigCommerce, and custom platforms.

Orders automatically create or update contacts, trigger follow-up sequences, and update deal stages. Abandoned carts become recovery campaigns. VIP customers get white-glove treatment.

03

Accounting Software Integration

Connect Zoho CRM with accounting systems for seamless quote-to-cash workflows. We integrate Zoho Books, QuickBooks, Xero, MYOB, and other leading platforms.

Quotes sync to invoices, payments update deal stages, customer balances display in CRM, and financial data informs sales strategy. Sales and finance finally work from the same data.

04

Payment Gateway Integration

Automate payment tracking and reconciliation by connecting payment processors to Zoho CRM. We integrate Stripe, PayPal, Square, Authorize.net, and other gateways.

Payments automatically update deal stages, trigger fulfillment workflows, and generate receipts. Failed payments alert account managers. Recurring revenue tracks automatically.

05

Marketing Platform Integration

Unify sales and marketing data by connecting your marketing automation platform to Zoho CRM. We integrate Mailchimp, HubSpot, ActiveCampaign, Klaviyo, and Zoho MarketingHub.

Campaign responses, email engagement, and lead scores flow into CRM. Sales sees exactly what content each prospect consumed. Marketing gets closed-loop attribution.

06

Custom API Integration

Every business has unique systems requiring custom connectivity. We build bespoke integrations using Zoho CRM APIs, webhooks, middleware platforms, and custom development.

Whether connecting to legacy systems, proprietary databases, IoT platforms, or third-party services, we design integrations that are secure, scalable, and maintainable long-term.
