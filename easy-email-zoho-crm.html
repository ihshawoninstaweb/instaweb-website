The Challenge

Email is how business gets done. But email history rarely lives in CRM where it should.

Sales and service teams communicate with customers primarily via email. Yet this valuable communication history often stays trapped in individual inboxes, invisible to the rest of the team.

When team members leave, customer context leaves with them. Handoffs require tedious forwarding and explanation. Managers can't coach based on actual conversations. Important commitments get lost in long threads.

Easy Email solves this by automatically capturing and organizing email communications inside Zoho CRM. Every email becomes part of the customer record, accessible to anyone who needs it.

Without email integration

  • Email history stuck in inboxes
  • Lost context during handoffs
  • No visibility for managers
  • Duplicate outreach to same contacts
  • Missing commitments and follow-ups

With email integration

  • Complete email history in CRM
  • Seamless team handoffs
  • Full visibility and coaching
  • Coordinated team communication
  • Tracked commitments and tasks
